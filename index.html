Rilès Kacimi - French rapper, producer and songwriter

Biography

Rilès Kacimi (born January 4, 1996) is a French rapper, songwriter and music producer. He gained popularity through his independent work, self-producing and releasing his songs.

Born in France to Algerian parents originally from Larbaa Nath Irathen, Rilès began making music at a very young age, learning to play the guitar and creating YouTube videos.

After earning his bachelor's degree, he decided to focus solely on music as a career. Rilès studied English literature at the University of Rouen, while studying music on his own in his spare time.

Rilès is also a talented painter. He sells his artwork as a way to earn extra money for his music career.
